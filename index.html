Hello!

My name is Mattia Guerini and I am assistant professor in econometrics at the University of Brescia.

I’ve obtained my PhD at the Sant’Anna School for Advanced Studies and I’ve then served as a post-doctorate for the OFCE-SciencesPo and as a Marie Sklodowska-Curie fellow at GREDEG, Universtity Côte d’Azur.

Research Interest

Topics: macroeconomics, industry dynamics, monetary economics.

Methods: agent-based models, econometrics, validation and calibration.
